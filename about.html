About the Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man

Peter Parker, also known as Spider-Man, is a teenage superhero who gained his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. As Spider-Man, Peter uses his super strength, agility, and ability to shoot webs to fight crime and protect the city of New York.

After gaining his powers, Peter struggled with the responsibility that came with them, leading him to make mistakes that ultimately cost him the life of his beloved Uncle Ben.

Peter has faced numerous challenges as Spider-Man, including battles with villains such as Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Sandman.
